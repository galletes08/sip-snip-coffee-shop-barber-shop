Vision

At Sip and Snip, we blend the art of grooming with the craft of coffee, creating a vibrant community hub. Our space offers high-quality haircuts and artisanal coffee, fostering connection and conversation. We aim to be the go-to destination for style and flavor, promoting creativity while providing a welcoming atmosphere for all.

Mission

At Sip and Snip PH, our mission is to provide an exceptional experience that seamlessly combines top-quality grooming services with expertly crafted coffee. We are dedicated to creating a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere where every customer feels valued and connected.

About Us

At Sip and Snip PH, we bring together two essential experiences—great coffee and exceptional grooming. Born from a passion for quality and community, our space is designed to offer you the best of both worlds. Whether you’re here for a morning pick-me-up, an afternoon unwind, or a stylish new look, we’re dedicated to providing top-tier service in a relaxed, welcoming environment. Our baristas craft each cup with care, using only the finest beans for rich, full-bodied flavors. Meanwhile, our expert barbers are here to make sure you look and feel your best, offering everything from classic cuts to modern styles and traditional shaves.
